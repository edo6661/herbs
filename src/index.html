Indonesia is the world's number one producer of palm sugar. Palm sugar is different from coconut sugar. Palm sugar is a sweetener made from pure male flower bunches of the sugar palm tree without a mixture of molasses. While coconut sugar/brown sugar is made from a mixture of coconut trees and molasses. Palm sugar has a distinctive and rich caramel flavor. We provide various forms of palm sugar such as cylinders, powder, and liquid which are of very high quality.
